About Confucius

Confucius (551-479 BCE) was an ancient Chinese philosopher and teacher whose ideas have profoundly influenced East Asian culture and thought. His philosophy emphasized personal and governmental morality, correctness of social relationships, justice, kindness, and sincerity. The Analects, a collection of his sayings compiled by his disciples, remains one of the most influential works in human history.
